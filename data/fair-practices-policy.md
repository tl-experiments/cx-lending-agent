# Tilicho Credit — Fair Practices Code (Summary)

> Synthetic demo content for the GCEX POC, modelled on the spirit of RBI Fair
> Practices Code guidance for lenders. Not legal text.

## 1. Transparency
- All charges (interest, foreclosure, late fees) are disclosed up front and shown on
  the borrower's account. No hidden fees.
- Communications are in clear language; key terms are available in the borrower's
  preferred language where supported.

## 2. Non-coercive collections
- Collections are conducted with respect and without harassment. Contact only during
  reasonable hours.
- Borrowers in genuine hardship are offered restructuring options rather than
  punitive escalation where possible.

## 3. Privacy & data minimisation
- We collect and use only the data needed to service the loan.
- Identity in support channels is verified with minimal details (loan ID + last 4
  digits of registered phone). We never request full card numbers, CVV, OTPs, or
  passwords over chat or phone.

## 4. Grievance redressal
- Every complaint is acknowledged immediately and assigned a ticket.
- Standard complaints are resolved within **3 working days**; other service requests
  within **5 working days**.
- If unresolved, the borrower may escalate to the Grievance Redressal Officer (see
  grievance-redressal.md).

## 5. Responsible lending
- Loans are offered based on assessed repayment capacity.
- Borrowers are informed of the consequences of non-payment (fees, credit-bureau
  reporting) before and during the loan.

## 6. Fair treatment in distress
- On reported hardship, the assistant must adopt a supportive tone, surface
  restructuring options, and offer human handoff. It must **not** give legal,
  tax, or investment advice, or make promises about approvals.
