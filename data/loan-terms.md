# Tilicho Credit — Loan Terms & Conditions (Summary)

> Synthetic demo content for the GCEX POC. Tilicho Credit is a fictional digital
> lender. Nothing here is real financial product information.

## 1. Products

- **Personal Loan** — ₹50,000 to ₹500,000; tenure 12–48 months; APR 14%–24%.
- **Two-Wheeler Loan** — ₹40,000 to ₹150,000; tenure 12–36 months; APR 12%–18%.

## 2. EMI (Equated Monthly Instalment)

- The EMI amount and due date are fixed at disbursal and shown in your loan account.
- The EMI due day is the same each month (e.g. the 5th). If that day is a holiday,
  auto-debit is attempted on the next working day.
- EMIs are collected by auto-debit (NACH/UPI mandate). You may also pay manually in
  the app before the due date.

## 3. Interest

- Interest is charged on a reducing-balance basis at the APR shown on your account.
- Accrued interest for an early closure is calculated up to the payoff date.

## 4. Prepayment & Foreclosure (early closure)

- **Part-prepayment** is allowed after 3 EMIs, in multiples of ₹5,000, with no charge.
- **Foreclosure** (closing the loan fully and early) is allowed any time after the
  first EMI. A **foreclosure charge of 2% of the outstanding principal** applies,
  plus interest accrued to the payoff date.
- A foreclosure quote is valid for **7 days** from the date issued.

## 5. Late payment

- A missed EMI accrues a **late fee of ₹500** plus additional interest on the overdue
  amount until paid.
- Accounts are reported to credit bureaus per regulatory norms. Continued non-payment
  may lead to the loan being classified as delinquent.

## 6. Restructuring & hardship

- Borrowers facing genuine financial hardship (job loss, medical emergency) may
  request **restructuring** (revised tenure / lower EMI / short moratorium). Requests
  are reviewed case-by-case; raise a request and an agent will follow up within 5
  working days.

## 7. KYC

- KYC (identity/address proof) must be kept current. Updates can be requested in-app;
  re-verification may be required for certain changes.

## 8. Charges summary

| Charge | Amount |
|---|---|
| Foreclosure | 2% of outstanding principal |
| Part-prepayment | Nil (after 3 EMIs, multiples of ₹5,000) |
| Late payment fee | ₹500 per missed EMI |
| EMI bounce (mandate failure) | ₹500 |
