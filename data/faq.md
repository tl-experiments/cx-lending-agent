# Tilicho Credit — Frequently Asked Questions

> Synthetic demo content for the GCEX POC.

**Q: How do I check my EMI amount and next due date?**
A: Your EMI amount and next due date are on your loan account. Ask the assistant with
your loan ID and it will look it up.

**Q: When is my EMI due?**
A: On the same day each month, fixed at disbursal. If it falls on a holiday, auto-debit
runs the next working day.

**Q: What happens if I miss an EMI?**
A: A late fee of ₹500 applies plus extra interest on the overdue amount until paid.
Repeated misses can affect your credit score and may make the loan delinquent. If
you're facing hardship, you can request restructuring.

**Q: Can I pay off my loan early?**
A: Yes. You can part-prepay (after 3 EMIs, in multiples of ₹5,000, no charge) or
foreclose the whole loan (2% of outstanding principal + interest to date). Ask for a
foreclosure quote with your loan ID.

**Q: How long is a foreclosure quote valid?**
A: 7 days from the date it's issued.

**Q: I lost my job / have a medical emergency. What are my options?**
A: You can request restructuring — a revised tenure, lower EMI, or a short moratorium.
The assistant can raise the request; an agent follows up within 5 working days.

**Q: How do I update my KYC?**
A: Request a KYC update in the app or ask the assistant to raise a KYC-update request.
Some changes need re-verification.

**Q: How do I make a complaint?**
A: Ask the assistant to raise a complaint, or use the grievance-redressal process.
Complaints are acknowledged immediately and resolved within the published SLA.

**Q: How do I change my auto-debit (NACH/UPI) mandate?**
A: Raise a request through the assistant and our team will share the steps; mandate
changes can take a few working days to activate.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: We only verify your identity using limited details (e.g. loan ID and the last 4
digits of your registered phone). We never ask for full card numbers, OTPs, or
passwords in chat.
