# Tilicho Credit — Grievance Redressal Process

> Synthetic demo content for the GCEX POC.

## How to raise a complaint
1. **Level 1 — Assistant / Support:** Ask the assistant to raise a complaint, or
   email support@tilicho-credit.example. You receive a ticket ID immediately.
2. **Level 2 — Grievance Redressal Officer (GRO):** If unresolved within the SLA, the
   complaint is escalated to the GRO.
3. **Level 3 — Regulatory ombudsman:** If still unresolved after the published
   timeframe, the borrower may approach the relevant banking/NBFC ombudsman.

## Service levels (SLA)
| Request type | Acknowledged | Resolved within |
|---|---|---|
| Complaint | Immediately (ticket issued) | 3 working days |
| Restructuring / hardship | Immediately (ticket issued) | 5 working days |
| KYC update | Immediately (ticket issued) | 5 working days |
| General service request | Immediately (ticket issued) | 5 working days |

## What we need from you
- Your loan ID and the last 4 digits of your registered phone for verification.
- A short description of the issue. The assistant can capture this and create the
  ticket for you.

## What we will never ask
- Full card numbers, CVV, OTPs, UPI PINs, or account passwords. Do not share these
  with anyone claiming to be from Tilicho Credit.
